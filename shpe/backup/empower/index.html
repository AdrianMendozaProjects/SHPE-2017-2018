High School Conference

Empower is a free all-day high school conference hosted by the Society of Hispanic Engineers chapter at UC San Diego that exposes students in grades 8-12 to career possibilities in the STEM fields.

Activities

The event will feature tours of UCSD engineering labs, keynote speakers, and various hands on engineering activities.

Free!

Empower is completely free! Make sure you register for this, spots are limited!

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the event?

EmPower 2015 will take place Saturday, April 25th, 2015 from 8-3 pm at the Jacob’s School of Engineering at UC San Diego.

Do I have to register to attend?

Yes! Your registration will confirm your spot and allow us to order enough food and shirts to accommodate to all of our participants. We do have a maximum capacity of 200 students so the sooner you register, the better.

Can I leave early?

Yes you may leave at any point of the conference; however, you will miss out on some of the activities planned.

What will the conference consist of?

The conference will consist of scheduled lab tours, a campus tour, hands-on engineering activities, as well as lots of interaction between you and UCSD students.

How old do I have to be?

Registration is open to students in grade 8 or above.

Can my little sibling come?

If your sibling is in grade 8 or above, yes.

Can my parents come?

Your parent is welcome to come, however, the conference is catered mostly for students in grades 8-12.

Will there be food?

Yes, free breakfast and lunch will be provided.

I have special diet needs. What should I do?

Let us know when you register. Click on “special dietary need” and let us know how we can accommodate to you.

Will there be transportation?

No, you will have to provide your own transportation to and from the conference. However, parking is FREE on weekends.

What if I don’t know anyone?

You will meet people here! Our many volunteers will be glad to get to know you.

Can I go without BOTH of my waivers?

No. Sadly, we cannot allow you to participate in the conference without both waivers signed by your parents.

I forgot my waivers. Can I still attend?

We will have a few extra waivers in case you forget yours at home. However, your parent must be with you at registration so he/she can sign them for you.

Where are the waivers?

Under the registration tab in the website. Don’t forget to print and sign BOTH of them
